# 🎨 Creative Analysis & Strategy Skill

> **Purpose**: Analyze competitor creatives, diagnose underperforming ads, generate creative improvement hypotheses, and write high-converting ad copy frameworks — all specifically calibrated for the Vietnamese market.

---

## 1. Creative Scoring System

When analyzing any ad creative (competitor or own), score on these 5 dimensions:

### 1.1 Hook Quality (0–20 points)
| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 18–20 | Stops scroll immediately, creates curiosity or pain point, highly specific |
| 14–17 | Good hook, but slightly generic or slow |
| 10–13 | Weak hook, requires reading past line 2 to understand |
| < 10 | No hook, leads with brand name or generic claim |

**Hook Formulas That Work in Vietnam:**
- `"[Pain Point] — Đây là lý do tại sao bạn vẫn chưa..."` (Problem → gap)
- `"Cảnh báo: Đừng [action] trước khi đọc cái này"` (Warning pattern)
- `"[Specific Result] trong [Timeframe] — Đây là cách"` (Result + how)
- `"[Controversial Statement]"` (Pattern interrupt)
- `"Sếp cần biết điều này trước khi chi thêm tiền quảng cáo"` (Direct address)

### 1.2 Offer Clarity (0–20 points)
| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 18–20 | Crystal clear value prop, specific numbers, unique differentiator |
| 14–17 | Clear offer, but lacking specificity or urgency |
| 10–13 | Vague offer, unclear what you get |
| < 10 | No clear offer, just brand awareness |

### 1.3 Social Proof (0–20 points)
| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 18–20 | Specific testimonial (name, location, result), user-generated content |
| 14–17 | Generic testimonial or star rating without specifics |
| 10–13 | Implied social proof ("thousands of customers") |
| < 10 | No social proof |

### 1.4 CTA Strength (0–20 points)
| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 18–20 | Specific, action-oriented, low-friction ("Claim free sample") |
| 14–17 | Clear CTA but generic ("Learn more", "Shop now") |
| 10–13 | Weak or missing CTA |
| < 10 | No CTA |

### 1.5 Format-Message Fit (0–20 points)
| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 18–20 | Format perfectly matches message (video for transformation, carousel for options) |
| 14–17 | Good format, but could be improved |
| 10–13 | Mismatched (e.g., complex info in static image) |
| < 10 | Wrong format entirely |

**Total Score:**
- **80–100**: 🟢 High-performing creative (scale if ROAS confirms)
- **60–79**: 🟡 Good with room to improve — test specific improvements
- **40–59**: 🟠 Mediocre — needs significant revision
- **< 40**: 🔴 Underperforming — replace entirely

## 2. Creative Fatigue Detection

Check these signals to determine if an ad creative is fatigued:

### Frequency-Based Signals
- Frequency > 3.0 within 7 days → Creative fatigue likely
- Frequency > 2.0 + CTR drop >30% week-over-week → Fatigue confirmed

### Performance-Based Signals (Concerning Pattern)
```
Week 1: CTR (all) = 2.5%, CPA = 180,000đ  ← Baseline
Week 2: CTR (all) = 2.1%, CPA = 210,000đ  ← Early fatigue
Week 3: CTR (all) = 1.6%, CPA = 280,000đ  ← Fatigue confirmed
Week 4: CTR (all) = 1.1%, CPA = 400,000đ  ← MUST REPLACE
```

### Engagement Rate Ranking Signal
- Drops from "Above Average" to "Average" → Fair warning
- Drops to "Below Average (Bottom 35%)" → Urgent creative refresh needed
- Drops to "Below Average (Bottom 20%)" → Stop ad immediately

## 3. Creative Improvement Hypotheses

When CTR (all) drops or CPA rises, test these hypotheses **one at a time**:

### High-Impact Tests (Test First)
1. **Hook Change**: Keep offer same, change first 3 seconds / first line only
2. **Thumbnail Change**: For videos, test different first frame
3. **Lead with Social Proof**: Lead with testimonial instead of product claim

### Medium-Impact Tests
4. **Format Toggle**: If static image → test short video (15s) and vice versa
5. **Audience Statement**: Add "Nếu bạn đang [problem]..." opening
6. **Urgency Add**: Add deadline or scarcity to existing offer

### Low-Impact Tests (Test Last)
7. **Color/Design Change**: Keep copy same, change creative design
8. **Length Toggle**: Long copy vs. short copy

**Rule**: Test only **one element at a time**. Wait for statistical significance (at least 100 impressions AND 3–5 conversion events per variant).

## 4. Vietnamese Market Copy Frameworks

### Framework 1: PAR (Problem → Agitate → Resolution)
```
[Sentence 1-2: State the PROBLEM specifically]
"Bạn đang tốn tiền quảng cáo Facebook nhưng không ra đơn?"

[Sentence 3-4: AGITATE — make the pain feel real]
"Mỗi ngày nhìn ads manager mà CPA cứ tăng, ngân sách cứ hao..."

[Sentence 5-6: RESOLUTION — your solution]
"[Product/Service] giúp bạn [specific result] trong [timeframe]."

[CTA]
"👉 Xem ngay tại [link]"
```

### Framework 2: STAR (Situation → Task → Action → Result)
```
[Situation]: Khách hàng của tôi đang [situation]...
[Task]: Họ cần [achieve X] mà không muốn [Y]...
[Action]: Vì vậy họ đã thử [product]...
[Result]: Kết quả: [specific measurable result] trong [timeframe]
```

### Framework 3: AI Hook + Social Proof + CTA
```
"[Surprising fact / stat about industry]

[2-3 lines: Why this matters to them]

[Name], [location]: "[Testimonial in quotes]"

[CTA button text → link]"
```

## 5. Video Ad Structure (for Vietnamese market)

### Short-Form (15–30 seconds) Structure:
```
0:00–0:03  HOOK: Show pain OR result (no logo, no intro)
0:03–0:10  PROBLEM: Agitate the pain OR show before/after
0:10–0:20  SOLUTION: Show product working briefly
0:20–0:25  SOCIAL PROOF: Quick testimonial text overlay
0:25–0:30  CTA: Clear action + benefit reminder
```

### Medium-Form (30–60 seconds) Structure:
```
0:00–0:05  HOOK (pattern interrupt)
0:05–0:15  PROBLEM development
0:15–0:30  SOLUTION demonstration
0:30–0:45  SOCIAL PROOF (testimonial video or text)
0:45–0:55  OFFER (specific deal)
0:55–1:00  CTA
```
